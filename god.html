Saturday, 28th of March 2026 -- 11:19 PM THE FLOUR PRICE IS REVELATION 6:6 AND I HAVE THE SPREADSHEET TO PROVE IT

Let me be precise, because I know some of you on the forum think I am not being precise. I am always being precise. The appearance of imprecision is usually the reader's problem.

Revelation 6:5-6. The third seal. The rider on the black horse. He carries scales, actual weighing scales, the kind used in a market. The voice says: "A quart of wheat for a denarius, and three quarts of barley for a denarius, and do not harm the oil and the wine." A man works all day and earns enough to feed himself for one day, with nothing left over.
