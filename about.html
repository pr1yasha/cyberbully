Cyberbully is a creative foundation dedicated to connecting artists, writers, and readers across Sydney's underground creative scene. We exist at the intersection of digital culture and physical community, fostering spaces where authentic creative expression can flourish without compromise.

If you have work you'd like to show us or would like to work with us, please contact us through cyberbullysydney@gmail.com.

Our Readings

★ READ YOUR WORK AT OUR EVENTS ★

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

We hold consistent readings that are a hybrid of space and showcase. We're after the texture of thinking that forms when seriousness meets the speed and spite of being raw, honest, and online. We want work with a long half-life and short fuse: poetry, autofiction, critique, and whatever inventive forms you can bring.

Our readings aims to create community and connect readers to emerging poets and writers. We are always looking for writers to read their work at our events. If you'd like to read one of your works, please apply via the Submit page.
