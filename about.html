Cyberbully is a hybrid of space and showcase. We‚Äôre after the texture of thinking that forms when seriousness meets the speed and spite of being raw, honest, and online. We want work with a long half-life and short fuse: poetry, autofiction, critique, and whatever inventive forms you can bring.

Cyberbully aims to bridge the gap and create community for creatives, writers, and readers with high aesthetic standards. We host readings that feature writers from the community and connect readers to emerging poets and authors. Selected works from each event are published into a zine.
